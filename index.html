Our Story

BUILT ON
GRIT


ProLivity Cleaning was born from a simple idea: that cleaning done right is one of the most powerful forms of respect — for a space, for the people who live or work in it, and for the craft itself.

Founded by a U.S. Navy veteran with roots in Jamaica, ProLivity brings together two powerful traditions — military discipline and island-born resilience. We're based right here in Arizona, serving our local community with the same standard of excellence we lived by in uniform.

We started small on purpose. Because doing it right matters more than doing it fast.
